Your donations are needed to support the Suppressed Histories Archives, which receives no institutional funding or support. If you see the importance of more people knowing the full story about women and all the interrelated issues covered on this site, you can help put it out there with a material contribution. Donations of any amount are received with thanks.

You can donate to the Archives in three ways:

• Donations by check or money order
If you don't need a tax deduction, and don't use PayPal, you can send a check or money order made out to Max Dashu, PO Box 3511, Oakland CA 94609, and earmark it for SHA (that is, the Suppressed Histories Archives). Please note: do not make checks out to the Archives! they can't be cashed unless made out to Max Dashu.

• Tax deductible donations
We are able to accept tax-deductible donations through our fiscal sponsor, the venerable lesbian journal Sinister Wisdom. Important: make the check out to Sinister Wisdom but be sure to write Suppressed Histories in the memo line. (Without this earmark, the donation goes to SW and not to the Archives. We are not able to process tax-deductible donations for checks not made out to Sinister Wisdom.)

• Donate online through a secure PayPal connection.
Major credit cards are accepted, you don't need to be a PayPal member. Just click on the Donation button below to make your contribution. (if you are donating by credit card, ignore the PayPal registration and click on the Continue button near the credit card icons).

For major supporters: Donors of $100 or more receive a Female Icons poster.

Donors of $500 or more will receive the Women's Power dvd, the Female Icons poster, any art print of your choice, and one hour's consultation (by phone or in person at the Archives).

Donors of $1000 or more will receive the Women's Power dvd, the Female Icons poster, a fine giclée digital print on canvas (art of your choice) and an hourlong consultation (by phone or in person at the Archives).

To receive these gifts of appreciation, please include your mailing address with your donation or by email.

Thanks for your support!

Max Dashu, director, Suppressed Histories Archives (founded 1970)
