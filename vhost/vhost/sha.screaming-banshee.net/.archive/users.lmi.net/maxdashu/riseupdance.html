I created this poster for an All Hallows Eve procession through the streets of San Francisco, convoked by Vicki Noble (who took the photo of dancing skeleton dakinis in Tibet). Along with Krissy Keefer, Starhawk & others,
we created a gathering of wild women and yoginis to call in the power of change and social justice on the eve of the 2008 election.

A very limited edition of signed prints is available for $25. (Only twenty of these collectibles remain, printed in vivid colors on heavy stock.)
