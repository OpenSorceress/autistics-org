Transdancer

Sacramental dance is an ancient path to inspiration
and divine union. It opens energy channels, joyfully
pouring light through the spine, limbs and organs.
It washes the soul and restores harmony.
Embracing the body as sacred, it reaches to
integrate the elements: earth, water, air, fire;
that is, body, emotions, mind, spirit.
