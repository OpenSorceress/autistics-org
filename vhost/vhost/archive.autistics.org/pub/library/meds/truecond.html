Autism Information Library

True Psychiatric Condition (Not Autism)

Non-situational anxiety or depression or other true psychiatric conditions (not autism!), that substantially get in the way of happiness or functioning I stress "non-situational", because some anxiety and depression is related to a child being in the wrong environment (which may include "being in school at all".) In these cases, it is more important to change the environment than to change the child. As far as "substantially gets in the way of happiness or functioning" this is also up to the person who would take the meds, to decide. It is not anyone else's right to say "You are unhappy" or "You are unable to function" "...and therefore you must take meds" regardless of how true or "obvious" this may seem from the outside.
