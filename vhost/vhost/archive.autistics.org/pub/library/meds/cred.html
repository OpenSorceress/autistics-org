My "Credentials"

In talking about these medications, it might be wondered what my "credentials" are.

Basically, my "credentials" are more valid than most doctors I know. I am autistic. I have been on many psychiatric medications (listed below). I have researched more medications than I have taken. I know many autistics who have been on psychiatric medications, some verbal, and some not, and have seen and heard the effects. I have had doctors and professionals tell me I know more about these medications than they do, and consult with me about their use.

As far as which medications (for psychiatric and some for neurological use) I have taken, they are the following (a partial list, since I have forgotten some undoubtedly, and since I am pretty sure I was not informed about some):

  • Adderall (dextroamphetamine/amphetamine) -- stimulant
  • Akineton (biperidin) -- antiparkinsonian
  • Ambien (zolpidem) -- sleeping medication
  • Ativan (lorazepam) -- sedative
  • Benadryl (diphenhydramine) -- antihistamine, used as sedative
  • Buspar (buspirone) -- sedative
  • Clozaril (clozapine) -- "atypical" antipsychotic
  • Cogentin (benztropine) -- antiparkinsonian
  • Depakote (divalproex sodium) -- anticonvulsant/"mood stabilizer"
  • Desipramine (desipramine) -- tricyclic antidepressant
  • Dexedrine (dextroamphetamine) -- stimulant
  • Dilantin (phenytoin) -- anticonvulsant
  • Effexor (venlafaxine) -- antidepressant
  • Haldol (haloperidol) -- antipsychotic
  • Klonopin (clonazepam) -- sedative/anticonvulsant
  • Lithium/Lithobid/Lithonate/etc (lithium carbonate) -- "mood stabilizer"
  • Luvox (fluvoxamine) -- SSRI antidepressant
  • Mazindol (mazindol) -- stimulant
  • Navane (thiothixene) -- antipsychotic
  • Neurontin (gabapentin) -- anticonvulsant
  • Paxil (paroxetine) -- SSRI antidepressant
  • Prolixin (fluphenazine) -- antipsychotic
  • Prozac (fluoxetine) -- SSRI antidepressant
  • Risperdal (risperidone) -- "atypical" antipsychotic
  • Ritalin (methylphenidate) -- stimulant
  • Seroquel (quetiapine) -- "atypical" antipsychotic
  • Serzone (nefazodone) -- antidepressant
  • Stelazine (trifluoperazine) -- antipsychotic
  • Symmetril (amantadine) -- antiparkinsonian
  • Tegretol (carbamazepine) -- anticonvulsant/"mood stabilizer"
  • Thorazine (chlorpromazine) -- antipsychotic
  • Trilafon (perphenazine) -- antipsychotic
  • Valium (diazepam) -- sedative
  • Wellbutrin (bupropion) -- antidepressant
  • Xanax (alprazolam) -- sedative
  • Zoloft (sertraline) -- SSRI antidepressant
  • Zyprexa (olanzopine) -- an antipsychotic

I am 19 years old. This is an incomplete list. Think about this for awhile.
