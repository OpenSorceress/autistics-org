Able to Discontinue Meds, At Any Time, For Any Reason

The person must be able to get off of the meds at any time, for any reason. The person taking the meds must have the choice and means to get off of the meds. They must understand about withdrawal or at least understand that the meds must be tapered off. In the case of a toxic reaction to meds in which they must be taken off immediately, they must have some way of being cared for (note: "cared for" does not mean "tied down") during withdrawal.
