Autism Information Library

Non-Standard Reactions in Autistics

Autistics do not react the "normal" way to many medications. This is because our brains are different.

Antipsychotics can cause us to hallucinate, impair our cognitive functioning in ways that are not fully understood or explained by the people who prescribe them, and can kill us. They have many long-term effects that are also rarely talked about.
