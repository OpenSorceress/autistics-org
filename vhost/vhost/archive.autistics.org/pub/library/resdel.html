Delayed Response to Speech

Because of delayed speech processing, I can sometimes take longer to respond to speech than I am "supposed to". I have been told by some people that sometimes I seem to sit there and visibly take awhile to process what they are saying before I respond. This could be partially because of CAPD, and partially because I sometimes have difficulty formulating words to respond with.

This can also lead to interrputing or overload, because I may be responding in the middle of the person's next sentence or paragraph.
